1.CONTACTING

  1. Identify yourself as a journalist/reporter
  2. Make it clear you’re working for Youth Radio
  3. State the story may be seen by a national audience
  4. Briefly state what the story is about
  5. Make it clear what you want (interview or background-only)
  6. Make it clear what the type of media (video, audio, photo, text)
  7. State your deadline
  8. Give your contact info
  9. Follow up via phone, email, text, etc. if you don’t hear back!
